Bhagat Singh
September 27, 1907 To March 23, 1931

Name : Shaheed Bhagat Singh.
Date of Birth : 28 September 1907.
Passed Away On : 23 March 1931.
Religion : Hindu.
Location : Banga, Punjab.
Address : Punjab.

Life Summary

Regarded as ‘Shaheed’ Bhagat Singh, Bhagat Singh is a popular Indian revolutionary. One of the most influential nationalist leaders of the Indian Independence movement, Bhagat Singh is remembered as the Indian revolutionary who laid down his life for the sake of independence. He was hanged on 23rd March 1931 with his fellow comrades Rajguru and Sukhdev.

Biography

“If the deaf are to hear, the sound has to be very loud. When we dropped the bomb, it was not our intention to kill anybody. We have bombed the British Government. The British must quit India and make her free."
Bhagat Singh is a popular Indian revolutionary. He is considered as one of the most influential nationalist leader of Indian Independence movement. Bhagat Singh is often referred to as ‘Shaheed’ Bhagat Singh. The word ‘Shaheed’ means a martyr.
